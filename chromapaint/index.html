ChromaPaint

ChromaPaint is a tool for OBS that allows people to draw stuff on stream. It requires Flash Player and BrowserSource to use it.

In order to add it to OBS, add a new Browser Source source, right click on the source you just added, go to properties, and insert the link below as the URL.

https://tehscratchguy.github.io/chromapaint/launchapp/

To interact with it, right click on the source and click on Interact, a window should pop up and you should be able to interact with it.

Controls

Use left click to draw

Press R to clear
